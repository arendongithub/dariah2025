Step 2 - Define the data schema

    Pydantic data objects

  • [Art Catalogue] = list of [Artworks]
  • An [Artwork] has properties:
    • title
    • type
    • a list of [provenances]
    • a list of [copies]
    • a list of [literature]
    • a list of [characters] depicted on the artwork
